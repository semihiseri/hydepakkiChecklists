Cottage Trip Checklist

    Gear:

  • Backpack
  • Sleeping bag
  • Sleeping mattress
  • Headlamp and batteries
  • Something to sit on

    • Clothing:

  • Hiking shoes
  • Hiking shirts and trousers
  • Rain and windproof jacket
  • Warm clothes (e.g. fleece, wool)
  • Sleeping clothes
  • Socks (spare and wool)
  • Cap, scarf, gloves

    • Eating:

  • Eating utensils (bowl, spork, mug, etc.)
  • Water bottle

    • Personal Hygiene:

  • Toilet paper
  • Toothbrush and toothpaste
  • Hand sanitizer

    • Sauna:

  • Towel
  • Shampoo, soap etc.

    • Miscellaneous:

  • Personal medicine
  • Personal food (i.e. snacks)
  • Powerbank
